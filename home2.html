OUR UNCOMPROMISING LEGACY

Built Without Compromise, Played Without Limits

We merge centuries-old Spanish acoustic geometry with cutting-edge modern CNC tolerances to create guitars that play effortlessly and resonate endlessly. Tone isn't something that can be mass-produced; it must be coaxed out of organic timber piece by piece.

When you commission a build with Artisan Guitars, you are entering a historical relationship between luthier and artist. We map your hand span, fret pressure, and picking aggressiveness to ensure the neck carve and fret dressing is utterly bespoke to you.

THE RAW MATERIALS

Access The Private Vault

A great guitar starts its life decades before it ever hits a workbench. Our selection of AAAAA grade Flame Maple, old-growth Honduran Mahogany, and CITES-approved African Ebony ensures that your guitar sounds exactly like it looks: stunning.

All woods are structurally tap-tuned and kiln-dried for up to 10 years before they are permitted to be carved. This extreme desiccation guarantees acoustic brilliance and zero future warping.

  • Sustained Reforestation sourcing
  • Hand tap-tuned frequency matching
