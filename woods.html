About Our Tonewoods

Only the Finest, Carefully Selected Materials

Every billet of wood in our vault has been hand-selected for its specific gravity and cellular structure. We source only the top 1% of FSC-certified tonewoods, which are then air-dried in our climate-controlled NY facility for a minimum of 5 years to ensure absolute stability and peak resonance before they ever meet a blade.

FSC Master-Grade Selection

We strictly adhere to CITES regulations and international sustainability standards, ensuring the ethical harvest of rare species like African Ebony and Brazilian Rosewood.

5+ Year Seasoning Program

Our wood is never kiln-dried to "speed up" production. We rely on the natural aging of resins to create the crystalline top end that defines an Artisan instrument.
